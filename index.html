GAME RULES

  1. Use the start button to start the round. Your player area is red.
  2. Choose your wepon by clicking a hand posture from your play area. (Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard or Spock)
  3. Your choice and the computer choice are revealed and the winer of the round ads a point to the score.
  4. After all the rounds are compleated, the higher score wins the game.
